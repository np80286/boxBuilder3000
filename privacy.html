What Box Builder stores today

The calculator stores some settings in your browser local storage so your last-used values can persist between visits. This is used for convenience and basic workflow continuity.

What Box Builder does not currently do

  • No account system is required to use the calculator.
  • No payment information is collected by the app.
  • No direct user-upload database is part of the current static-site version.

Future advertising and analytics

If advertising or analytics are added later, this page should be updated to reflect the services used and any relevant browser or third-party data behavior. If ad networks are enabled, they may use cookies or similar technologies according to their own policies.

Your choices

If you want to clear stored calculator values, you can clear local storage for this site in your browser or use the in-app reset tools where available.
