When To Use A Wedge Box

A wedge can be the right answer for fitment, but it is worth understanding what changes when you build one.

Calculator All Guides About

Why builders choose a wedge

A wedge box can match sloped rear seats or awkward hatch geometry better than a simple rectangular cabinet. That can help preserve space while still reaching the target enclosure volume.

Where mistakes happen

  • Forgetting that top depth and bottom depth change usable internal volume.
  • Assuming the wedge shape automatically fits the vehicle better without measuring the actual contact points.
  • Ignoring driver depth or port clearance when the shallow side gets too tight.

Use the calculator for the hard part

Box Builder helps translate wedge dimensions into more useful enclosure numbers, but you should still verify physical fit and panel layout before cutting any material.
